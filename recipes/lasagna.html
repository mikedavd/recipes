Lasagna

Description

Layers of sexy pasta with tomato sauce, mince and white sauce/cheese topping.

From Italy, obviously

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mince
  • White Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Get the pasta sheets
  2. Add the mince and sauce
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Cook
  5. Lasagna!
